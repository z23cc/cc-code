<HARD-GATE>
Do NOT design or build anything until all 5 questions are answered.
</HARD-GATE>

## Workflow

1. Ask Q1: What problem are you solving? (demand)
2. Ask Q2: How are people solving it now? (status quo)
3. Ask Q3: What specifically makes your approach better? (specificity)
4. Ask Q4: What's the smallest version you could test? (wedge)
5. Ask Q5: What have you observed that others missed? (insight)
6. Score each dimension 1-5, total out of 25
7. Produce a 1-page design brief
8. Recommend next step based on score
